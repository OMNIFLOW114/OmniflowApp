The OmniFlow Story

Founded on October 10, 2025, OmniFlow was born from my experience as a JKUAT Economics student who recognized the need for an inclusive e-commerce platform that serves everyone. We've evolved into Kenya's most comprehensive marketplace platform serving sellers of all sizes.

Our Platform Philosophy

OmniFlow is designed as an all-inclusive marketplace where big sellers can scale their operations, small businesses can grow their reach, and students can learn entrepreneurship in a dedicated, supportive environment.

Key Innovations

Smart Same-Day Delivery

Our intelligent system prioritizes products closest to the buyer in the marketplace. This ensures faster delivery, lower costs, and a better shopping experience.

Simple Legal Store Setup

Create your store in minutes! Just fill the required fields on our Create Store page and submit only the requested documents.

Lipa Mdogo Mdogo

Our revolutionary buy-now-pay-later system designed for all Kenyans. Break down payments into manageable installments.

Zero Commission Stores

Free for the first 1,000 sellers! Keep 100% of your profits while accessing our growing customer base.

Student Marketplace Section

Dedicated platform within OmniFlow for students. Exclusive deals, campus delivery, textbooks exchange, and entrepreneurship opportunities.

Enterprise Analytics

Advanced tools for big sellers including sales analytics, customer insights, and inventory management to help scale your business.

Local-First Marketplace

Our algorithm ensures buyers see products available near their location first. This boosts local businesses, reduces delivery time, and cuts carbon footprint.
