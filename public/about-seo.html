The OmniFlow Story

Founded on October 10, 2025, OmniFlow was born from my experience as a JKUAT Economics student who recognized the need for an inclusive e-commerce platform that serves everyone. While we started with a focus on students, we've evolved into Kenya's most comprehensive marketplace platform serving sellers of all sizes - from large enterprises to individual entrepreneurs, with a dedicated Student Marketplace section.

Our Platform Philosophy

OmniFlow is designed as an all-inclusive marketplace where big sellers can scale their operations, small businesses can grow their reach, and students can learn entrepreneurship in a dedicated, supportive environment. We believe in democratizing e-commerce for all Kenyans.

Who Uses OmniFlow?

Our platform is built to serve the complete spectrum of Kenyan sellers and buyers:

Big Sellers & Enterprises

Scale your business with our advanced analytics, bulk listing tools, and corporate account features. Reach millions of customers across Kenya with our intelligent local-first algorithm.

Small Businesses & Entrepreneurs

Start and grow your online store with zero commission for the first 1,000 sellers. Access our Lipa Mdogo Mdogo payment system to increase your sales and customer reach.

Student Marketplace Section

A dedicated space for students to buy affordable items, sell products, and learn entrepreneurship. Exclusive student discounts, campus delivery networks, and peer-to-peer trading.

Key Innovations

Smart Same-Day Delivery

Our intelligent system prioritizes products closest to the buyer in the marketplace. This ensures faster delivery, lower costs, and a better shopping experience for everyone.

Simple Legal Store Setup

Create your store in minutes! Just fill the required fields on our Create Store page and submit only the requested documents. We handle the legal complexities for you.

Lipa Mdogo Mdogo

Our revolutionary buy-now-pay-later system designed for all Kenyans. Break down payments into manageable installments to increase purchasing power.

Zero Commission Stores

Free for the first 1,000 sellers! Keep 100% of your profits while accessing our growing customer base. Competitive rates for high-volume sellers.

Student Marketplace Section

Dedicated platform within OmniFlow for students. Exclusive deals, campus delivery, textbooks exchange, and entrepreneurship opportunities.

Enterprise Analytics

Advanced tools for big sellers including sales analytics, customer insights, and inventory management to help scale your business effectively.

Local-First Marketplace

Our algorithm ensures buyers see products available near their location first. This boosts local businesses, reduces delivery time to hours instead of days, and cuts carbon footprint by minimizing transportation distances.
