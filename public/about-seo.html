About Me

I'm Kirangi John Njeru, a BSc Economics student at Jomo Kenyatta University of Agriculture and Technology (JKUAT) and the Founder & CEO of OmniFlow Technologies.

OmniFlow Story

Founded on October 10, 2025, OmniFlow was born from my experience as a student struggling with expensive and complicated e-commerce platforms. I built OmniFlow to make online buying and selling simple, affordable, and accessible for every Kenyan - especially students and small businesses.

Key Features

  • Lipa Mdogo Mdogo: Revolutionary buy-now-pay-later system
  • Zero Commission Stores: Free for the first 1,000 sellers
  • Same-Day Delivery: Across major Kenyan cities
  • Student Marketplace: Exclusive deals for students
  • OmniCash Wallet: Secure digital payments
