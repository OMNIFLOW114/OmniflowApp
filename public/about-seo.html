The OmniFlow Story

Founded on October 10, 2025, OmniFlow was born from my experience as a JKUAT Economics student struggling with expensive and complicated e-commerce platforms. I built OmniFlow to make online buying and selling simple, affordable, and accessible for every Kenyan - especially students and small businesses.

Key Innovations

Smart Same-Day Delivery

Our intelligent system prioritizes products closest to the buyer in the marketplace. This ensures faster delivery, lower costs, and a better shopping experience for everyone.

Simple Legal Store Setup

Create your store in minutes! Just fill the required fields on our Create Store page and submit only the requested documents. We handle the legal complexities for you.

Lipa Mdogo Mdogo

Our revolutionary buy-now-pay-later system designed specifically for students and young entrepreneurs. Break down payments into manageable installments.

Zero Commission Stores

Free for the first 1,000 sellers! Keep 100% of your profits while accessing our growing customer base.

Student Marketplace

Exclusive deals, discounts, and opportunities for students. Buy affordable, sell easily, and build your business while studying.

OmniCash Wallet

Secure digital payments with end-to-end encryption. Send, receive, and store money safely within the platform.

Local-First Marketplace

Our algorithm ensures buyers see products available near their location first. This boosts local businesses, reduces delivery time to hours instead of days, and cuts carbon footprint by minimizing transportation distances.
